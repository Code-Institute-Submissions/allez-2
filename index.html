Welcome Kopites

Hello and welcome to the page. This, after Anfield is the second home of the champions of Europe and the greatest football team to ever exist. This page was created as a one stop hub where anyone can come and learn about the past of one of the most successfull domestic and international teams of all time. Our journey will take us through each of Liverpools 6 European cups and will give an overview of the match dramas, goals, tactics, results and teams. While this page will highlight the greatest moments in the clubs history it does not go over the many struggles and heartaches the club has had to endure over the years. On the brink of despair the fans kept singing through it all and as if beckoned to succeed by sheer force of will the team always follwed suit. From the beginning of this club the tenet has been to always believe. To never walk off the pitch with regrets. That it's never too late to make a change and that at the end of a storm there's a golden sky. So without further ado please see the section below where you may navigate to the real meat of the page.
